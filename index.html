The Solution

Deal Document Intelligence Agent

An AI-powered deal assistant that knows exactly which documents every deal needs, validates them instantly when they arrive, and flags the exact fields that are missing or incorrect — before they stall your pipeline. Think of it as a dedicated operations specialist embedded in every deal, catching the issues that normally surface weeks later during implementation.

Smart Checklists, Zero Guesswork

The system generates the exact document list based on deal configuration — number of employees, EINs, state requirements. No more generic templates or missed forms.

Validation in Seconds, Not Days

Uploaded documents are scanned instantly for missing signatures, blank required fields, and formatting issues. The WOTC agreement missing a phone number? Caught in seconds, not discovered during implementation.

Multi-EIN Complexity, Simplified

Complex deals with multiple entities get individual tracking per EIN. See exactly which entity is complete and which needs attention — all in one view.
